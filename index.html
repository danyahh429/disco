Medicaid

For the DC area, there are three Manage Care Organazations that are included in DC medicaid. These MCOs are organizations that seek to reduce healthcare expenditure costs. The organizations for the DC area are Medstar, CareFirst under Blue Cross Blue Shield, and AmeriHealth.

Qualifications

First and foremonst you must understand the federal poverty level and how it is detemined as it serves for the basis of qualification. The FPL is determined based on the presumed average cost of living for Americans. This is ultimately based on monthly income and family size with the two having a direct relationship as determining factors. As of 2022, someone who is meets the FPL will gross at least $13,590 annualy, or about $1,132 monthly.
